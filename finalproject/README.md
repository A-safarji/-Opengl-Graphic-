
# Opengl-Graphics
Opengl in C/C++ for xcode mac

## Tips for final project
* D= distortion
* P= prospect
* Zero= lightings off
* 1 to 4=lights and shadow 
* T= multi textuers 
* 5= sky mapping  
* F= freeze animation  
* Multiple colors by clicking the menu and choose the color.
